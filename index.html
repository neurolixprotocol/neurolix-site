About

What is Neurolix Protocol

A vertical DePIN protocol built specifically for AI teams operating on regulated data.

The Problem

Healthcare, finance, and biotech cannot use modern AI because public cloud providers offer no cryptographic proof that sensitive data stays private.

Our Approach

Neurolix Protocol runs AI workloads inside hardware-secured enclaves (AMD SEV). Every session produces an attestation anchored on Base L2.
