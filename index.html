About

What is Neurolix Protocol

A vertical DePIN protocol built specifically for AI teams operating on regulated data — healthcare, finance, biotech, and legal.

The Problem

78% of enterprises cite data privacy as the #1 barrier to AI adoption. Healthcare, finance, biotech, and legal sectors cannot use modern AI on their most valuable data because public cloud providers offer no cryptographic proof that sensitive data stays private.

Our Approach

Neurolix Protocol runs AI workloads inside hardware-secured Trusted Execution Environments (AMD SEV, Intel TDX). Every compute session produces a cryptographic attestation token, verified by the provider and anchored on Base L2.

Our Vision

In the coming years, no serious enterprise will train AI on sensitive data without cryptographic proof of confidentiality. Neurolix Protocol is building that infrastructure layer.

Building in Public

We document every milestone. The protocol is in active development. Every iteration and architectural decision is published openly on our Build Log.

Architecture

Three Foundational Pillars

Built on hardware guarantees, verified by cryptographic attestation, anchored on a public blockchain.

🛡️

Confidential Compute

AI workloads run inside hardware-secured TEE enclaves where memory is encrypted at the silicon level. No data exposure to anyone.

🔗

On-Chain Attestation

Every compute session produces a cryptographic commitment hash, anchored on Base L2. Tamper-proof and immutable audit trail.

🏥

Regulated AI Ready

Designed for sectors where data privacy is a legal requirement. GDPR and HIPAA compliance built into the protocol primitive.
