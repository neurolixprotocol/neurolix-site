About

What is Neurolix Protocol

A vertical DePIN protocol built specifically for AI teams operating on regulated data — healthcare, finance, biotech, and legal.

The Problem

78% of enterprises cite data privacy as the #1 barrier to AI adoption. Healthcare, finance, biotech, and legal sectors cannot use modern AI on their most valuable data because public cloud providers offer no cryptographic proof that sensitive data stays private.

The bottleneck is not compute power. It is the absence of mathematical proof.

Our Approach

Neurolix Protocol runs AI workloads inside hardware-secured Trusted Execution Environments (AMD SEV, Intel TDX, AWS Nitro). Every compute session produces a cryptographic attestation token, verified by the cloud provider and anchored on Base L2.

The result: enterprises gain a tamper-proof audit trail of AI computation on regulated data — without ever exposing the data itself.

Our Vision

In the coming years, no serious enterprise will train AI on sensitive data without cryptographic proof of confidentiality. Neurolix Protocol is building the infrastructure layer that will make regulated AI possible at scale.

Building in Public

We document every milestone — successes and unfinished edges. The protocol is in active development. Every iteration, every audit finding, every architectural decision is published openly. Trust is earned through transparency, not promises.

Architecture

Three Foundational Pillars

Built on hardware guarantees, verified by cryptographic attestation, anchored on a public blockchain.

🛡️

Confidential Compute

AI workloads run inside hardware-secured TEE enclaves where memory is encrypted at the silicon level. No data exposure — not to the cloud provider, not to the network operator, not to us.

🔗

On-Chain Attestation

Every compute session produces a cryptographic commitment hash, anchored on Base L2. Tamper-proof audit trail, publicly verifiable, immutable. Mathematics replaces institutional trust.

🏥

Regulated AI Ready

Designed for healthcare, finance, biotech, and legal — sectors where data privacy is a legal requirement. GDPR and HIPAA compliance built into the protocol primitive.
